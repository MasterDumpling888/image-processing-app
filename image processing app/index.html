How to use

This app allows you to apply filters to your snapshots!

To use this, pressed "s" to take a snapshot of your webcam!

You can also use apply face filters by pressing:

  • "g": to apply a grey scale filter
  • "b": to apply a blur filter
  • "c": to apply a color filter
  • "p": to apply a pixelate filter
  • "m": to apply a mask
You can choose want mask you want to apply through the option box!
